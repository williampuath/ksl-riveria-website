Development Overview

Riveria Garden @ Wawari is a visionary 1,026-acre integrated township meticulously developed to offer residents a harmonious blend of luxury, comfort, and natural beauty. Strategically located within the core growth corridor of Iskandar Puteri, the development enjoys prime positioning and exceptional long-term growth potential. The masterplan features a comprehensive mix of landed residences, high-rise developments, and commercial components — thoughtfully designed to create a modern, self-sustaining township that integrates living, commerce, and leisure.
